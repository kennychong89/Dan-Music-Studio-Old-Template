About Dan's Music Studio

At Dan's Music Studio, we provide a warm, bright, clean, and welcoming environment for the instruction of music. All of our instructors are also working professional musicians who understand what it takes to perform at the highest level of excellence.

We offer lessons on most instruments and vocals to students of all ages. Piano guitar, vocal, and drum lessons are quite popular. Lessons are avaliable on most band and orchestral instruments and help to strengthen the skills of students involved in scholastic music programs. Additionally, we offer lessons on many folk instruments like fiddle, banjo, mandolin, and uklele.
